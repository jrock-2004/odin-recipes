Description

This is the greatest lasagna recipe you have ever experienced. This recipe has been handed down from one generation to another. I really hope you will enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Spices
  • Optional: Meat (ground beef or sausage works fine)

Directions

  1. Start boiling some water. Once your water is boiling, throw in your noodles. Cook for as long as the box says.
  2. While your water starts boiling, start cooking your meat if you decided to use this.
  3. Gather your sauces and spices.
  4. When the pasta and meat are done, it is time to layer everything in a 9x13 casserold dish. Make layers alternating between (a) sauce, ricotta cheese, meat and (b) the cooked lasagna pasta
  5. Add some cheese to the top
  6. Bake this at 350 degrees until the top is golden brown
  7. Don't forget to let it cool
